## ALTERNATIVE PROTECTION STRATEGIES

### **Copyright (Immediate):**
- Register designs with Copyright Office
- Protects specific artistic expressions
- Automatic upon creation (registration strengthens)
- Cost: $45 online filing

### **Design Patent:**
- Protects ornamental design of functional items
- Useful for unique glass shapes
- Cost: $2,000-3,000
- Term: 15 years from grant

### **Trade Dress:**
- Protection for product packaging/design
- Requires secondary meaning
- More expensive to enforce
- Consider after market establishment
