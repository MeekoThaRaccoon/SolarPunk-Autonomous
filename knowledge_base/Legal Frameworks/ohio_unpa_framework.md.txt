## OFFICIAL OHIO REVISED CODE CHAPTER 1745

### **Verified Statutes (Current as of Jan 2026):**

#### **§ 1745.01 Definitions**
- **Unincorporated nonprofit association:** Two or more persons joined by mutual consent for a common, nonprofit purpose
- **Member:** Any person who, under rules or practices of association, may participate in selection of persons authorized to manage affairs or conduct activities
- **No filing required with Secretary of State**

#### **§ 1745.35 Liability of members, managers, and persons acting on behalf of association**
(a) A person is not liable for a breach of an unincorporated nonprofit association's contract or for a tort or other act or omission solely because the person is a member, is authorized to manage the affairs of the association, or is a person acting on behalf of the association.

text

#### **§ 1745.02 Formation**
- **Formation:** By two or more persons agreeing to form an association
- **No articles of incorporation required**
- **No filing fees**
- **No state registration**

#### **§ 1745.03 Bylaws**
- "An unincorporated nonprofit association may adopt bylaws that contain provisions for managing and regulating the affairs of the association."
